Jaehwi Jang

My work is centered on the areas of learning from demonstrations, robust perception, and control, with a focus on creating robots that are user-friendly and require minimal instruction for everyday individuals. My latest projects involve learning constraints from the demonstrations. I am pursuing my Master's degree at KAIST, under the guidance of Daehyung Park. This follows my achievement of a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and Physics from the same institution.
